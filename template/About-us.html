Who are we?

Greenwish Futsal is located in Kapan (Kathmandu). It is a 5v5 futsal facility opened with an aim to promote the sport of futsal and to establish the branches around Kathmandu. It was started in 2016 AD with a motive to provide a convenient hangout place. It also includes a gymnasium and a swimming pool for recreational activities with affordable price.
